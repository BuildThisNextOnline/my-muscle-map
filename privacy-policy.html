Privacy Policy

My Muscle Map (MMM) · buildthisnextonline.github.io/my-muscle-map

My Muscle Map is a strength tracking tool built by BuildThisNextOnline. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is stored, and how it is used.

The creator cannot see your data

The creator of My Muscle Map has no access to any data you enter into the app. This is by design:

  • All statistics and insights shown in My Muscle Map are generated using only the data you have entered yourself
  • Data stored in your browser's localStorage lives entirely on your own device — no one, including the creator, can access it remotely
  • Data stored in Google Drive is saved to a spreadsheet in your own Google Drive account — the creator has no server, no database, and no mechanism to access it
  • The app has no backend — there is no server that receives, processes, or stores your information

Your body metrics, workout history, and personal profile are yours alone.

What data the app collects

My Muscle Map collects only the data you choose to enter:

  • Your name, biological sex, date of birth or age, height, and bodyweight — entered during profile setup
  • Workout sessions — exercise name, set type, weight, reps, RIR, and computed strength scores
  • Body metrics — weight, BMI, body fat percentage, and other optional scale measurements you choose to log

The app does not collect any data automatically. No usage analytics, no crash reports, no device identifiers, and no location data are collected.

How your data is stored

By default, all data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage on your device. Nothing is sent to any server by default.

If you choose to sign in with Google, the app additionally stores your data in a Google Spreadsheet created in your own Google Drive. This enables cross-device sync — your data on your phone and laptop stays in sync through your own Google account.

The app requests only the drive.file scope, which means it can only access files it has created itself. It cannot read, modify, or delete any other files in your Google Drive.

Google account access

When you sign in with Google, the app:

  • Reads your name from your Google account solely to label your spreadsheet
  • Reads your email address to identify you as a user of My Muscle Map
  • Creates one Google Spreadsheet in your Drive named MMM - My Muscle Map - [Your Name] - [Date] onwards
  • Reads from and writes to that spreadsheet to sync your workout and body metrics data across your devices

Your email address is stored by BuildThisNextOnline in a private registry for the purpose of informing you about future tools and offerings from Build This Next. It is not sold or shared with any third party.

By signing in with Google, you agree that Build This Next may contact you about future tools and offerings. You may opt out at any time by emailing privacy@buildthisnext.online and your email will be removed from the registry.

The app does not access your Gmail, Google Calendar, Google Photos, or any other Google service.

You can revoke the app's access to your Google account at any time by visiting myaccount.google.com/permissions and removing My Muscle Map.

Data sharing

Your data is never sold, shared, or transmitted to any third party. It lives either in your browser's localStorage or in your own Google Drive — both of which are under your control.

Data deletion

To delete your locally stored data, clear your browser's site data for this domain.

To delete your Google Sheets data, simply delete the spreadsheet from your Google Drive. To revoke the app's Google access, visit myaccount.google.com/permissions.

Anonymous user counting

When you sign in with Google, the app generates a random anonymous identifier (UUID) and records it — along with the date — in a registry maintained by BuildThisNextOnline. This is used solely to count how many distinct users have signed in to My Muscle Map.

This UUID is randomly generated and contains no personal information. It is not linked to your name, email address, Google account, or any data you have entered. It cannot be used to identify you.

The UUID is stored in your browser's localStorage under the key mmm_uuid. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

This app uses GoatCounter for privacy-friendly, cookie-free page view analytics. GoatCounter does not track individuals, does not use cookies, and does not collect personal information. Only aggregate page view counts are recorded.

Children's privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, the updated date below will reflect the change. Continued use of the app after a policy change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
